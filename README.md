# Car Sales Performance Report with PowerBI

>[!NOTE]

>Dataset is taken from Kaggle (Cleaned and Additional Information Added via DAX & M)

>PowerBI pulls live data from Excel Sheet, please refresh data to update visual contents. 



>[!NOTE]

>This is a personal project showcasing my skills in Data Analytics and Storytelling.



## Skills Performed

1. ETL Processing.
2. Data Transformation via DAX and M Query.
3. Data Visualization and Data Storytelling Flow.
4. Drill through and Slicer referencing.
5. Creative Data Layout with Data Storytelling in mind.



## Dashboard Goals and Key Objectives

1. Main Page 

* Who are the top dealerships?
* Who are the top brands?

2. Dealership Page 

* Which brand is the most sought after? 
* Which model is the most sought after? 
* What is the current sales performance? 
* Who are their clients and most evident customers? 

3. Brand Page

* What are the top models? 
* Where are the demands coming from? 
* What engine and transmission type is most evident?
* What is the Income-to-Price ratio of their customers? 
* What is the price action of their top models?
* What are the top models per region?
