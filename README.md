# Car Sales

Business Dashboard for Car Sales performances



This dashboard aims to answer the following guide questions:



Main Page

* Who are the top dealerships?
* Who are the top companies?

Dealership Page

* Which brand is the most sought after?
* Which model is the most sought after?
* What is their current sales trend?
* Who are their customers?

Brand Page

* What are their top models?
* Where is the most demand?
* What type of engine and transmission is most evident?
* What is the price action on car models?
* What is the Income-to-Price ratio of their customers?
